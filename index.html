Case File: The Poudel Family Incident

Late last night, a disturbance was reported at the Poudel household. Neighbors noticed unusual activity, but each family member insists they were elsewhere. Among them was a mysterious figure seen near the house.

The family: sucpou (elder daughter), prabster (younger daughter), mummypou (mother), baba poudel (father), and an outsider, Bob. One of them is hiding the truth, but not all clues point directly to the culprit.

Your job as the detective is to carefully examine their alibis and statements. Choose wisely.

Alibis

sucpou: Claims she was reading in her room all night.
prabster: Says she was helping her mother in the kitchen preparing snacks.

baba poudel: Went for a walk around the block. No one saw him return until after midnight.
bob: A delivery driver who claims he was only driving nearby and never stopped at the house.
